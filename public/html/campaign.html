Prickly Pear Extract

As your body tries to break down alcohol, it inevitably produces free-radicals that can damage your cells. Prickly Pear Extract has been used for centuries to protect your proteins and lipids from damage when your body breaks down alcohol.

Milk Thistle

Home to the planes of Southern Europe, Milk Thistle is an herb revered by traditional cultures for it's anti-oxident and anti-inflammatory properties. Milk thistle, and its silymarin component, increases the activity of your antioxidant enzymes to combat the free-radicals produced when your body breaks down alcohol.

Thiamine

Commonly known as Thiamine, B Vitamins improve signs of alcohol intoxication by reversing the depletion of your protective enzymes as your body breaks down alcohol. B vitamin intake may also prevent the depletion of glutathione, an antioxidant that protects you from oxidative stress.
