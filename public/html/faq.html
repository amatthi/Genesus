How do I track my order?

Track your order's progress by visiting your dashboard and inputting your unique Order Number. Through our portal, you'll be able to track if your order has been printed and shipped – and we'll give you estimates for each step of the way.

What about returns and refunds?

If, for any reason, you are not satisfied with the order you receive, please contact us. One of our representatives will be happy to help make things right within 30 days of receiving your order.

How do I cancel my order?

If you decide that you no longer want your order for any reason you can cancel it. ​Once a campaign ends, however, we are unable to cancel an order as the information has already been sent to the manufacturer and the item will be shipped anyway. In this instance, you can return your item once you receive it for a full refund.

When are you charged?

All sales transactions take place once the minimum number of orders required to manufacture the campaign have been met. This number is generated by a wide variety of factors, so it will differ between campaigns. Generally, though, the minimum tends to be between 3-5 orders.

This will always be one-time charge, no matter the circumstance.

How did Genesus begin?

Genesus was born on a whim and founded by Alek and Austin Matthiessen while the two were seniors at Brown University and Emory University. After using nutrition products for 10 years, they realized most of the formula's were the same and wanted to build their own brand. The process was horrific, sending quotes back and forth and putting up thousands of dollars with no guaranteed sales. They knew there had to be a better way, so they built one.

The Genesus platform allows people to take their own personal brand and experience, create various supplements, and then sell them without the overhead costs or restrictions usually associated with starting an online store. Our platform sells the items in campaigns (or limited edition runs) so we can manufacture and ship in bulk. This process keeps our costs low and allows us to help build small brands by offering fair and profitable pricing on manufacturing and products to our sellers.

How safe is your production?

We've partnered with manufacturers all of the United States to produce supplements we can believe in. Each of our facilities is cGMP certified, FDA Registered, and makes all of their raw ingredients in the United States of America. Each of our products is also 100% transparent and free of any additives or fillers. As standard procedure we test all incoming raw materials, as well as finished products, and test each batch for disintegration time.

We also use amber colored recyclable glass bottles preserve freshnessglass bottles, labels list all ingredients to avoid consumer allergic reactions to unnamed ingredients, Best By dates guarantee freshness, and full body, tamper-evident sleeves ensure consumer safety.

How do I know if a product is Gluten Free?

Since the beginning, we’ve gone one step further in assuring the gluten free status of our products by testing our finished products for gluten. We confirm that none of the products we offer contain more than 10 ppm gluten, and use this logo on the front panel of the label. Our program provides a consistent, science-based set of standards that helps consumers easily choose our premium supplements. In mid-2013 the FDA announced a decision defining Gluten Free as 20ppm, more than double what we allow in products. All finished products undergo gluten testing using an ELISA (Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay) procedure (a machine that detects the presence of an antigen in a sample) to detect the presence of gluten.

How do I know if a product is Organic?

We believe offering organic alternatives to dietary supplements provides the end consumer with a wholesome product that supports the cycle of global sustainability and thus adds value for our retail accounts. All our products are nestled in plant-based biodegradable packaging materials and shipped in corrugated cartons with an overall recycled content of 65% (considered to be the highest recycled content possible while maintaining box integrity during packing and shipping). More than half of our products meet the guidelines of the USDA National Organic Program.

In the US, you are required by law to file a Form 1099 if you receive more than $600 in profits from Genesus. Trademark

The concept behind trademark rights is that the owner of the mark has the legal right to stop people from trading off of the value of the company’s brand and reputation. They can do so by stopping the use of any confusingly similar trademark.

So if an unknown car company wants to use the mark “VMW” to identify their new car, BMW will be able to stop them from doing so based on the similarity of the marks themselves and the goods they are used to identify.

Often trademark owners will also secure rights in other related goods that they want to sell – for example, supplements.

Copyright

Under copyright law, you are not entitled to copy someone else’s copyrightable work. You cannot digitally copy someone’s original creative expression, manually trace it, or create your own digital or freehand copy of it. You have to originate your own works, creating them yourself.

For example, in one famous case, a photographer was found to have copied the photograph of another artist even though he used a different model and location. The original creative details of the second photograph were so similar to the first (the look, layout and subject matter) that the court found copyright infringement.
