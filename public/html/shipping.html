SHIPPING

Genesus currently ships to all residential and commercial addresses in the Continental U.S. We do not currently ship to P.O. Boxes, Alask, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, or Canada, but hope to do so in the near future.
Our orders ship within 24 to 72 hours of when they are placed. You will receive a shipping confirmation with tracking information once your order ships.
Shipping and handling costs may vary depending on the rates offeredby our shipping carriers and the promotions we may run. We do our very best to provide fast, economical shipping to our customers.
